About Us

Your business deserves to flourish. We provide the tools to help you make sure it does.

Why Us

Businesses often spend too much time on admin and not enough performing the core function. When introducing software, often the cost and complexity of the hardware and software is far too high.

We’re changing that by providing tools that make life easier and less costly - our app helps you manage your jobs, staff and customers.

Whenever you make a sale, we provide multiple payment options including using our chip and pin reader - one of the best in class. You can also send invoices and we’ll keep track of them until paid.

We’re here to empower businesses of all sizes and help them compete in the wider market.

Our Story

Our founder George Johnson was losing sales because he was spending too much time on business admin and had no way of taking payments. It was time to do something about it.

We quickly got a small team together and created an app with features that help small businesses across the UK manage sales, reach more customers and accept payments on-the-go.

We are helping more people grow their businesses every day. Our team is growing rapidly and we are continuing to innovate and introduce new features.
